Our Mission

What We Do

Grassroots initiatives across education, healthcare, nutrition and social awareness — reaching those who need it most.

📚

Free Education

Coaching classes, books and stationery for children from low-income families across rural Uttar Pradesh.

🍲

Food Distribution

Weekly meal drives serving daily wage workers, homeless individuals and underprivileged households.

🏥

Medical Assistance

Free health check-up camps and medicine distribution in remote villages with limited healthcare access.

💦

Women Empowerment

Skill development, legal awareness and livelihood support programmes for women in need.

🌿

Environment

Tree plantation drives and cleanliness campaigns building a sustainable future for coming generations.

👪

Social Awareness

Campaigns on child rights, voter education, sanitation and community harmony across the region.
